About

Background

MS in GIS & Natural Resource Management. 15+ years as a GIS Analyst in Houston, Texas. Volunteered teaching QGIS, Python, and ESL at universities in Sudan before the war. Now creating open-source geospatial training for the world.

Mission

Make professional GIS automation accessible where resources are limited. From watershed management to site selection — using only free, open-source tools. Teaching practical workflows that solve real problems.

Featured Project

QGIS AI Site Selection

Automated 6-criteria site selection analysis using PyQGIS processing algorithms. From 4,708 buildings to 9 suitable sites in one command. Generates interactive dashboard with data table, exports, and PDF report.
